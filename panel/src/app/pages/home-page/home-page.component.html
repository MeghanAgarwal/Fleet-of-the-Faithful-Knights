Fleet of the Faithful Knights

Many centuries ago, during the Middle Ages, the Holy Order of Knights committed themselves to the safe passage of pilgrims to the Holy Land. They dedicated their lives, fortunes, and sacred honor to this cause. They were Holy Military Orders that, through force, brought peace and security to the land.

Eventually, the Holy Orders were disbanded. Many of the leaders were executed; others were tortured. Their fortunes were confiscated, and all seemed lost to the Holy Orders. And for 1,500 years their remnants remained in the shadows.

Now today, in the new frontier, a new threat challenges all of mankind. The threat of the Vanduul, of Pirates, and thieves abound. The Holy Order of Knights has re-emerged to face this threat head-on, without fear or hesitation. The Fleet of the Faithful Knights is a beacon of light in a dark universe. They stand tall with Honor and Bravery. The Faithful Knights provide protection, security, and stability to a Universe with so little in it.

Make no mistake, the Fleet of the Faithful Knights will NEVER shy away from battle. If you are Honorable, you will welcome us. If you are also Brave, then you will wish to join us. And, if you are an enemy of the pure, the righteous, and the kind, then YOU WILL FEAR US.
